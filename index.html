MileProof Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

MileProof by Landeros Systems does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All trip data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any remote servers for user data storage.

Purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store payment information.
